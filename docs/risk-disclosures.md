# Risk Disclosures

**Please read these disclosures carefully before using this platform.**

By enabling live trading, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and accepted all risks described in this document.

---

## 1. No Guarantee of Profit

The TON AI Agent platform provides algorithmic trading tools. **There is no guarantee that any agent or strategy will be profitable.** All AI and algorithmic trading systems carry the risk of significant financial loss.

- Historical or simulated performance is not indicative of future results.
- Past profits in simulation do not guarantee profits in live trading.
- Market conditions change, and strategies that performed well historically may underperform in the future.

---

## 2. Risk of Total Loss

**You may lose all of the funds you allocate to an agent.** Only invest capital that you can afford to lose entirely. Do not invest funds needed for essential expenses such as rent, food, medical care, or debt repayment.

---

## 3. Cryptocurrency Market Risks

Cryptocurrency markets are highly speculative and volatile:

- **Price volatility:** Crypto assets can gain or lose 10–80% or more of their value in a single day.
- **Liquidity risk:** In low-liquidity conditions, orders may be executed at significantly worse prices than expected (slippage).
- **Market manipulation:** Crypto markets are susceptible to pump-and-dump schemes and other forms of manipulation.
- **24/7 markets:** Trading occurs continuously, including during periods when you are unable to monitor your agent.
- **Regulatory risk:** Government actions, including outright bans on crypto trading, can cause rapid price declines.

---

## 4. Smart Contract Risk

This platform interacts with smart contracts on the TON blockchain. Smart contracts may contain:

- **Undiscovered bugs** that could result in loss of funds.
- **Exploitable vulnerabilities** that malicious actors may target.
- **Unexpected behavior** under rare or extreme market conditions.

Even contracts that have been audited by third-party security firms can contain vulnerabilities. An audit does not guarantee the safety of a contract.

---

## 5. Platform and Technology Risks

- **Software bugs:** The platform software may contain errors that cause unintended trading behavior.
- **Downtime:** The platform may be unavailable due to maintenance, technical failures, or cyberattacks, preventing you from stopping agents during a market event.
- **API failures:** Connectivity issues with external exchanges or price feeds may cause delayed or failed orders.
- **Data errors:** Inaccurate market data may cause agents to make poorly-timed trades.

---

## 6. Authentication and Account Security Risk

Your Telegram account is the sole method of authentication on this platform. If your Telegram account is compromised:

- An attacker could start, stop, or modify your agents.
- An attacker could attempt to move funds controlled by your agents.

**It is your responsibility to secure your Telegram account.** Enable Two-Factor Authentication. Review your active sessions regularly. Never share your authentication credentials.

---

## 7. Regulatory Risk

- This platform is **not licensed or regulated** as a financial service provider in most jurisdictions.
- It does not provide investment advice, financial advice, or regulated financial services.
- Using this platform may be restricted or prohibited in your jurisdiction. It is your responsibility to determine whether use of this platform is legal where you are located.
- There is **no investor protection scheme** covering your use of this platform.
- We do not provide tax reporting. You are responsible for reporting any profits or losses to relevant tax authorities.

---

## 8. No Fiduciary Duty

The platform does not act as your financial advisor, broker, or fiduciary. All trading decisions executed by agents are based on the strategies and configurations you set. The platform makes no representations about whether any strategy is suitable for your financial situation.

---

## 9. Counterparty and Custody Risk

- Funds allocated to agents remain in your wallet until an agent places a trade. Once a trade is executed on-chain, the transaction is **irreversible**.
- The platform does not hold or custody your funds. You retain control of your wallet and are responsible for its security.
- Third-party DEX protocols and liquidity pools used for trade execution carry their own risks, including protocol exploits and rug pulls.

---

To the maximum extent permitted by applicable law, the platform, its developers, and affiliates are not liable for:

- Any financial losses arising from use of the platform.
- Losses due to smart contract bugs, market manipulation, or force majeure events.

- Indirect, consequential, or punitive damages of any kind.
