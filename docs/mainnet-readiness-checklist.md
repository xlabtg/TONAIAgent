# Mainnet Readiness Checklist

> Complete every item on this checklist before enabling live trading with real funds.
> This checklist is designed to protect you from common mistakes that new users make.

---

## Section 1 — Account Security

- [ ] **Telegram 2FA enabled**
  Enable Two-Step Verification in Telegram: *Settings → Privacy and Security → Two-Step Verification*

- [ ] **Telegram recovery code saved**
  Store your 2FA recovery code offline (e.g., printed or in a password manager). If you lose access to your account, you lose access to your agents.

- [ ] **Active Telegram sessions reviewed**
  Remove unknown sessions: *Settings → Privacy and Security → Active Sessions*

- [ ] **Telegram account email set**
  Add a recovery email to your Telegram account for account recovery.

---

## Section 2 — Wallet Readiness

- [ ] **Dedicated trading wallet created**
  Create a separate TON wallet exclusively for agent trading. Do not use your primary savings wallet.

- [ ] **Wallet address verified**
  Double-check your TON wallet address character by character. A single typo can result in permanent fund loss.

- [ ] **Wallet backup secured**
  Your seed phrase is stored securely offline. Never store it digitally or share it with anyone.

- [ ] **Test transaction completed**
  Send a small test transaction (e.g., 0.1 TON) to and from your trading wallet to confirm it works correctly.

- [ ] **Wallet permissions reviewed**
  Review which applications and contracts have permissions to interact with your wallet.

---

## Section 3 — Platform Understanding

- [ ] **Simulation mode understood**
  I have read the [User Security Guide](./user-security-guide.md) and understand the difference between simulation and live trading.

- [ ] **Risk disclosures read**
  I have read the full [Risk Disclosures](./risk-disclosures.md) document and accept the risks.

- [ ] **Strategy behavior understood**
  I understand how my chosen strategy behaves, including when it buys, sells, and what triggers a stop-loss.

- [ ] **Emergency stop procedure known**
  I know how to immediately stop my agent:
  1. Open the Mini App → Agents → tap agent → Stop Agent Permanently

---

## Section 4 — Simulation Review

- [ ] **Minimum simulation period completed**
  My agent has run in simulation mode for at least 7 days (14 days recommended).

- [ ] **Simulation trade history reviewed**
  I have reviewed all simulated trades and the strategy is behaving as expected.

- [ ] **Simulation drawdown acceptable**
  The maximum drawdown in simulation is within my acceptable risk tolerance.

- [ ] **Simulation ROI reviewed**
  I understand that simulation performance is not a guarantee of live performance.

---

## Section 5 — Risk Configuration

- [ ] **Risk level set to `low` for first 30 days**
  Start conservative. You can increase your risk level after 30 days of successful live trading.

- [ ] **Initial capital allocation set to 1–5%**
  Allocate only a small fraction of your portfolio for the first month.

- [ ] **Maximum drawdown limit configured**
  Set a drawdown limit you are comfortable with. The agent will stop automatically if this limit is reached.

- [ ] **Daily loss limit set** (if available)
  Configure a daily maximum loss to prevent runaway losses.

---

## Section 6 — Monitoring Setup

- [ ] **Telegram notifications enabled**
  Enable trade notifications so you are alerted immediately when your agent executes trades.

- [ ] **Daily check-in plan in place**
  Plan to review your agent dashboard at least once per day during the first week.

- [ ] **Emergency contact saved**
  Know how to reach platform support in case of issues.

---

## Section 7 — Final Acknowledgment

Before enabling live trading, confirm each statement:

- [ ] I have completed all sections of this checklist.
- [ ] I understand that AI agents can and do lose money.
- [ ] I am only investing funds I can afford to lose entirely.
- [ ] I have not been pressured or incentivized to enable live trading before I feel ready.
- [ ] I am not relying on this platform as my primary source of income.
- [ ] I understand that crypto markets are available 24/7 and my agent may trade at any time.
